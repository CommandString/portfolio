About Me

The start of my computer endeavor

I got my first computer at 6 years old, a Dell Latitude with Windows XP. After 3 years of getting used to computers and learning a decent bit of how to use them, my father taught me how to build a desktop. Funny story because we accidentally used a dead motherboard, but after I turned the computer on I knew from that moment that I loved computers. About a year later he opened up a business for repairing computers, phones, etc and I mostly did computer jobs as I didn't even have a phone at that point but eventually we did have to close down the business (for reasons irrelevant to the business itself).
